Grace Path Wellness Center – Elevated Outpatient Behavioral Health Website

Prompt:

Design a high-end, modern, and discreet outpatient behavioral health website for:

Grace Path Wellness Center
Serving Fairfax County and Prince William County, Virginia.

This is NOT a residential rehab.
This is an outpatient and intensive outpatient behavioral health clinic serving adults.

The brand must feel:

• Private
• Clinical
• Structured
• Calm
• Refined
• Trustworthy
• Professional
• Insurance-ready
• Court & hospital referral appropriate

Avoid:
• “Rehab” clichés
• Emotional stock photos
• Group circle hand-holding imagery
• Overly spiritual tone
• Bright colors
• Loud gradients

The design should resemble:
A concierge psychiatric or medical clinic in Tysons Corner or McLean.

🎨 Brand System
Color Palette

Primary Background:

Accent:
Muted Sage – #7C8C7A

Secondary Neutral:

Dark Footer:

No bright blues. No red. No loud green.

Typography System

Headlines: Elegant serif (Canela-style or refined editorial serif)
Subheadings: Medium-weight serif
Body: Clean sans serif (Inter, Avenir, or Helvetica Neue style)

Typography must feel editorial and spaced intentionally.

Large margins.
Generous white space.
Strong hierarchy.
